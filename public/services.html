Rentals

Need a cab for multiple stops? Our rental packages give you flexibility and convenience for all your travel needs. Perfect for shopping trips, tourist sightseeing, business meetings, or any occasion when you need a vehicle at your disposal.

  • Flexible hourly packages (4, 8, or 12 hours)
  • Fixed pricing based on time, not distance
  • Make unlimited stops within your rental period
  • Option to extend your rental on-the-go
  • Choose from various vehicle types based on your needs
  • Dedicated driver stays with you throughout your journey
