About GoWheels

Driving you forward with reliable and affordable transportation solutions since 2018.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, GoWheels began with a simple mission: to revolutionize the way people travel within and between cities. What started as a small fleet of 10 cars in Mumbai has now grown into one of India's most trusted cab services, operating in over 30 cities nationwide.

Our founder, Bhavesh, experienced firsthand the challenges of finding reliable transportation during an emergency. This personal experience inspired him to create a service that prioritizes reliability, safety, and customer satisfaction above all else.

Over the years, we've continually evolved our services to meet the changing needs of our customers, embracing technology and innovation to deliver a seamless booking experience across all platforms.
