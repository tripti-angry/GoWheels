Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a cab with GoWheels?

+

You can book a cab through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service. Simply enter your pickup and drop location, select the type of service and cab, choose the date and time, and confirm your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, UPI, and cash payments. You can choose your preferred payment method during the booking process.

How can I track my driver's location?

+

Once your booking is confirmed and a driver is assigned, you can track their location in real-time through our mobile app. You'll also receive SMS notifications with driver details and estimated arrival time.

Can I cancel my booking? Is there a cancellation fee?

+

Yes, you can cancel your booking through our app or website. Cancellation fees may apply depending on how close to the pickup time you cancel. Cancellations made more than 30 minutes before pickup usually don't incur any charges.

Do you offer corporate accounts for businesses?

+

Yes, we offer corporate accounts with special features like centralized billing, employee transportation management, custom travel policies, and a dedicated account manager. Please contact our business team for more details.
